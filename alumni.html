AEPi Stands With Emory


October 6

This past Sunday morning the Brothers of the AEPi Epsilon Chapter at Emory University woke up to find swastikas spray painted on the outside of their house. This occurred shortly after the conclusion of Yom Kippur, the holiest day of the year on the Jewish calendar.

We the members of the AEPi Sigma chapter at Washington University in St. Louis do not stand for these acts of hatred against our brothers and are taking action to raise awareness. Whatever the chapter, we are all AEPi and stand together as one.

We would like to nominate the AEPi Delta Chapter (University of Illinois), AEPi Tau Chapter (Vanderbilt University) and the AEPi Omega Deuteron Chapter (University of Michigan) to join us in our support of our fellow brothers and hope that they will continue to spread this message. We Stand with Emory.
