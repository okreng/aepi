AEPiBetaPhi Wins the Burmeister Cup!


April 19

Over the past several months the brothers of Alpha Epsilon Pi, in conjuction with the sisters of Pi Beta Phi, had been working tirelessly to build a facade and produce a play for the annual Thurtene Carnival.

Shown above is the facade, which was built under the leadership of Seniors John Drescher, Steve Evans, Carly Lippman, and Janey Asher. Our theme was "City Block".

Inside, ten plays were shown over the weekend. The play featured an evil mayor who shut down the city's subways, and his determined deputy mayor who thwarts his evil plan. The play was directed by Seniors Oliver Krengel and Sophie Graham.

On Sunday night, Thurtene honorary announced that AEPiBetaPhi was this year's recipient of the Burmeister cup for overall excellence in facade building and production. This was the first time we had won in over ten years!
